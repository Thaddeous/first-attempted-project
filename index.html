5 Brews to Try and Buy

#1 - Stone Arrogant Bastard Ale

Brewed By: Stone Brewing Co.

Self-Defined Style: American IPA

Strength (ABV): 7.2% ABV

Best With: Barbecue, ribs and traditional Indian or Thai food.

#2 - Highland Gaelic Ale

Brewed By: Highland Brewing Co.

Self-Defined Style: Celtic Red Amber Ale

Strength (ABV): 5.6% ABV

Best With: Anything! This ale is exceptionally balanced.

#3 - Dogfish Head 90 Minute IPA

Brewed By: Dogfish Head

Self-Defined Style: Imperial IPA

Strength (ABV): 9.0% ABV

Best With: Pork chops, beef, grilled fish and Stilton Cheese

#4 - Rogue Dead Guy Ale

Brewed By: Rogue

Self-Defined Style: Maibock

Strength (ABV): 6.5% ABV

Best With: Brothy soups, salted almonds, game birds and seafood.

#5 - Magic Hat #9

Brewed By: Magic Hat

Self-Defined Style: Not Quite Pale Ale

Strength (ABV): 5.1% ABV

Best With: Best enjoyed with spicy foods and stinky cheese.
